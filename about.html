Our Story

Skulbus was created out of a simple but powerful idea — to make school transport safer, more efficient, and easier to manage for schools and parents across Kenya.

Frustrated by the lack of accountability in traditional school transport systems, we built a digital platform that connects schools, drivers, and parents in real time. Today, Skulbus helps ensure that thousands of students get to and from school safely with tools for tracking, reporting, and communication.

From rural communities to urban centers, Skulbus is bridging the gap between technology and education logistics — and we’re just getting started.

Our Mission

To simplify and secure school transportation across Africa by providing schools and families with a smart, transparent, and reliable transport management platform.

Our Vision

To become the leading school mobility platform in Africa, setting the standard for safety, innovation, and convenience in education transport systems.
